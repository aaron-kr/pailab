## Prerequisites

Track 02 or equivalent. Python proficiency required.

## Units

1. Robot kinematics: forward and inverse
2. ROS 2 architecture and communication patterns
3. URDF and robot description
4. Perception: depth cameras and point clouds
5. Object detection and pose estimation on-device
6. Trajectory planning
7. PID and model-predictive control
8. Force/torque sensing and compliance
9. Sim-to-real transfer with Isaac Sim
10. Multi-robot coordination
11. Safety, e-stop design, and fail-safes
12. Capstone: autonomous pick-and-place system
