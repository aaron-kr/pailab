## Available datasets

### KASD-1k
Korean Ancient Script Dataset — 3,400 annotated glyph images from archival scans.
Classes: 40 Hunminjeongeum characters. Format: PNG + COCO JSON.

### GSRC-6
Gesture Recognition for Robotic Control — 1,200 samples across 6 gestures,
captured at 30 fps on an Arduino Nicla Vision. Format: MP4 + CSV labels.

### EPIB-edge
Edge-device Power and Inference Benchmark — latency, accuracy, and power draw
measurements for 12 TFLite models across 5 hardware targets.
