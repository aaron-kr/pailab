This paper investigates the use of convolutional neural networks for automated
recognition of ancient Korean script (Hunminjeongeum-era glyphs). A custom
dataset of 3,400 annotated character images was assembled from archival scans.
Fine-tuned EfficientNet-B0 achieves 91.4% top-1 accuracy on the held-out test set.

## Dataset

The KASD-1k dataset is available on Hugging Face under CC-BY 4.0.
