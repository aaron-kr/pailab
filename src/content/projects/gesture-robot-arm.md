A 4-DOF robot arm driven entirely by hand gestures captured via an Arduino Nicla
Vision running an on-device MobileNet classifier. No PC required — inference,
kinematics, and servo control all run on the microcontroller.

Built as both a research platform and a teaching demonstration for the
"Robotic Systems and Control" curriculum track.
