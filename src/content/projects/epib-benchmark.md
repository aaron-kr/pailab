Systematic benchmarking of TFLite and ONNX models across five common Physical AI
target boards: Arduino Nicla Vision, ESP32-S3, Raspberry Pi Zero 2W,
Raspberry Pi 4B, and NVIDIA Jetson Nano. Measures latency, accuracy, peak RAM,
and idle/active power draw under standardised workloads.
