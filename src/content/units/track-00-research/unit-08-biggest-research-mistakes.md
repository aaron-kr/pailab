### 🚫 Research Problems

- No clear research question
- No real data → just a project, not research
- No meaningful analysis

### 🚫 Writing Problems

- Methods/Results written as bullet lists
- Too much background in Introduction
- Not enough depth in Related Work
- Writing for the wrong audience (conference vs journal)

### 🚫 Structural Mistakes

- Adding figures/tables in the Conclusion
- Missing limitations or future work
- Ignoring required paper format/template

### 🚫 Citation Mistakes

- Wrong citation style
- Incomplete references
- Not enough sources
- Trusting AI-generated references without verification

### 🚫 Tool Misuse

- Letting AI write your paper
- Not verifying sources (use databases like Scopus)
