## Who it's for

Circuit theory I or II courses. Students are already learning Ohm's law, RC
circuits, and op-amps — this module shows how those fundamentals scale up into
the NPU silicon powering every smartphone and edge AI device.

## Schedule (1 week)

**Mon/Wed** — From transistors to multiply-accumulate (MAC) arrays; systolic array architecture  
**Fri** — Power analysis of inference hardware; Roofline model; lab: measure current draw of RPi running inference vs. idle
