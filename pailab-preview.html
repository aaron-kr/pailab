About

AS

Aaron Snowberger

Principal Investigator

aaron.kr aaronsnowberger.com kspai.org Naver blog (한국어)

The Physical AI Laboratory is a research group based in Jeonju, Korea, working at the intersection of machine learning, embedded hardware, and physical systems. I teach across five Korean universities and run an open research practice — papers, datasets, and code are published publicly wherever possible.

I'm also a founding organiser of KSPAI (한국피지컬AI학회), the Korean Society for Physical AI, and I believe the best research comes from building things — not just writing about them.
