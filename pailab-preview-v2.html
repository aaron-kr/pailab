The Physical AI Laboratory is a research group based in Jeonju, Korea, working at the intersection of machine learning, embedded hardware, and physical systems. I teach across five Korean universities and run an open research practice — papers, datasets, and code are published wherever possible.

I'm also a founding organiser of KSPAI (한국피지컬AI학회), and I believe the best research comes from building things — not just writing about them. My background is in education technology and linguistic computing, which is why I think about Physical AI through both an engineering and a learning lens.

Wyoming native. Laid back. Will talk robotics over coffee.
